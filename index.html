🔒 Privacy First

Your data stays on your phone.
Always.

SubTrack has no internet permission. Your SMS, your subscriptions, and your spending data never leave your device.

🚫
No Internet Permission
The app's AndroidManifest declares zero internet permissions. It is physically incapable of sending data anywhere.
📱
On-Device Processing
All SMS parsing happens locally in Kotlin. No cloud function, no API call, no SDK that calls home.
🗄️
Local Database Only
Subscriptions are stored in an encrypted Room (SQLite) database on your device. Only you can access it.
👁️
You Review First
Nothing is auto-saved. You see every detected subscription and choose what to import. You're always in control.
🔓
No Account Required
No email, no password, no profile. SubTrack works the moment you install it — no sign-up gate ever.
🗑️
Delete Anytime
Uninstalling the app removes all data. There's no server-side copy because there's no server at all.
