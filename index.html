SMS Detection

Your bank already knows.
Now you will too.

Every time you're charged for a subscription, your bank sends you an SMS. SubTrack reads those messages locally on your device and automatically builds your subscription list — with the exact amount you paid.

Netflix, Spotify, Hotstar, Amazon Prime, Google One, Swiggy One and 40+ more services are recognised automatically.
